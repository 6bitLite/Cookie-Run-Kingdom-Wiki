Star Coral Cookie

A lone lighthouse on a secluded island shines its guiding light across the Silent Ocean. Star Coral Cookie, the dedicated lighthouse keeper, warmly greets sailors from far and wide. Her shadow, cast upon the lighthouse stairs, resembles a coral emerging from the waves. It is said that the light in the lighthouse is special, taken from the legendary Giant’s Great Ocean, as told in the stories of the stars. But could that be true? Star Coral Cookie sings the star's story with conviction in her eyes, claiming she sailed across the Garden of the Queen, navigated through the Swirl guarded by the Four-legged Beast, and finally reached a library containing all knowledge. Although no Cookie believes her fantastical tale, it’s enough for her that the stars above know the truth.
