Shield of Conviction

Sound the charge! Hollyberry Cookie charges forward with her Shield of Conviction. While her Shield of Conviction is active, she will absorb a portion of the damage allies take, excluding periodic and indirect damage. Afterwards, she will buff ally Cookies, reducing incoming CRIT DMG and making them resistant to DMG debuffs.

Hollyberry Cookie will gain the Seed of Life buff depending on the amount of damage received during Shield of Conviction. Once she gains enough Seed of Life stacks, it will bloom into Berry of Life. Steelbound Passion will activate depending on the amount of damage received during Shield of Conviction or after a certain period of time. Steelbound Guardian will activate once Hollyberry Cookie has gained enough stacks of Steelbound Passion or when her HP drops below a certain level.

Entering the Steelbound Guardian state will reset Hollyberry Cookie's Cooldown, restore HP, and enhance Shield of Conviction. Her Final Hit will be buffed depending on the damage received during Shield of Conviction. The Cooldown for Hollyberry Cookie's first skill activation is reduced, and while she is using her skill, she will briefly become resistant to interrupting effects.
