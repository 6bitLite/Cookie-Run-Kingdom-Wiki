Doughael

The High Forkbearer and overall leader of the St. Pastry Order, Their Eminence took on the holy name Doughael long ago upon being shown the Godly Will in a Rift in the sky. Transforming in body and in spirit, They displayed thorough pastoral tenderness all while pursuing an agenda of reviving the Holy Avatar. As of the events of Premonition of Doom, They are deceased, having assimilated with the Avatar.
