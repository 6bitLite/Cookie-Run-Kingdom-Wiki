Awakened Holly Berry Cookie

"Sound the charge!" With her trusty shield aloft, Hollyberry Cookie's mighty presence alone can instill courage and confidence on the battlefield. In the past, not only did she unite the ever-squabbling Berry Houses, but she was also always be the first to extend a friendly hand, earning the Hollyberrians' unconditional trust and respect. And yet, no matter how strong the hero is, no one can carry the crushing weight of responsibility alone. Little by little, her heart, hidden behind the steel of her shield, began to show cracks until there was no other option left but to run away. But even so, Hollyberry Cookie's will to protect her dear ones never faded. Clad in her new armor, the hero raises her shield once again. Begin the battle song! Hollyberry Cookie has returned.
